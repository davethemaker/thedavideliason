Hi, I'm David. I was a paramedic after the 9/11 disaster, then a social worker working with developmentally disabled folks, and now I'm a programmer.

I'm most interested in the Javascript universe, as a full-stack developer. A solid foundation of vanilla JS is supplemented with express, mongoDB, mongoose, and ejs templating engine, as well as standard front-end tools of jQuery, Bootstrap, webpack, and more. I'm currently focusing on React and Native for building MERN apps.

With Johnny-Five, I'm able to explore my interest in combining JS with embedded electronics and how they can be used to create artistic and real-life soutions for problems in the real world.

I also enjoy bicycle touring, hiking, running, and reading.
