About

David Eliason is a technologist, entrepreneur, and project manager. He drives partnerships for tackling social problems through community building, empowerment and appropriate technology.

David became passionate about living a life focused upon helping others after a summer volunteering to build a community center in one of India's poorest areas.

David's first career path was providing emergency medical assistance as a paramedic, inspired by the heroic first responders of 9/11 disasters. While recovering from a motorcycle accident, David completed his graduaate studies in social justice and community development, following which he worked for GGRC. He worked with a non-profit organization for over eight years, dedicated towards improving the lives of persons with developmental disabilities, developing a number of community resources to allow for independent living. Working with persons with developmental disabilities, living in Silicon Valley with a number of tech companies, David felt like there must be room for creating more solutions to help people. While helping a family member with a health crisis, David began his new career in technology

David founded WeMakeGood Inc., a non-profit tech company, which specializes in developing free and open source software for changing how information flows in the world. He has been Executive Director since November 2016, strategically spearheading WeMakeGood to be translated into over various languages, a number of deployments and a global populations reach. He has been instrumental in increasing business development revenue and philanthropic capital into WeMakeGood.

He is on the founding team of Gudbot Inc, a hardware company that had developed the GudbotOne - designed and engineered as a rugged, self-powered, mobile Wi-Fi device which connects people and things to the internet in areas of the world with poor infrastructure. The company mission is to build products that will empower individuals, especially those with developmental disabilities.

His mission is to make things, collaborate with others, and help others built upon a foundation of compassion.

David is focused upon making good, helping others, being part of positive solutions
