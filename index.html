About

David Eliason is a technologist, entrepreneur, and project manager. He drives partnerships for tackling social problems through community building, empowerment and appropriate technology.

At the age of seventeen, David volunteered to spend a summer building a community center in one of the poorest areas of India. Witnessing extremes of poverty, and being part of a collaborative and proactive solution, set the passionate foundation upon which he would build his life work.

Inspired by the heroic first responders of the 9/11 disaster, David trained to become a paramedic and worked on the front-lines of helping suffering individuals within some of the most marginalized areas of SF and Silicon Valley.

A motorcycle accident opened a new door for David to follow his passion of helping others - while recovering, he completed a graduate program which empahsized both the sensitivity to problems within the community, as well as being able to create effective solutions. This academic preparation was followed by eight years of project management experience at Golden Gate Regional Center, a non-profit located in San Francisco with the mission to empower individuals with developmental disabilities to lead fuller lives within the community. This position created numerous opportunities to work within a variety of roles, from policy-making, collaboration with a wide array of stakeholders on both statewide and local levels, and team leading on dozens of resource development projects.

Working closely with persons with developmental disabilities, David believed that the same innovation that spurred nearby tech companies to build products could be harnessed to create powerful tools to help individuals and communities to meet their potential. While caring for a family member, he learned how to program and build solutions on a direct basis.

His mission is to make things, collaborate with others, and help others built upon a foundation of compassion. Using the skills of web development, IoT, and project managmenet, David hopes to continue to work towards making the world a better place.
